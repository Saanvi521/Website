INDIAN CULTURE

Saanvi Tyagi

"India" A country which most people know about. But what makes India so different from other nations, what makes it so special? Most people know that India is the second-most populated country in the world, is part of Asia, and is home to several of those around the world. But not many know that India's true identity comes from its diversity and cultural expression. Not many know that India is in fact home to around 447 languages. Not many know that Indians love to celebrate all occasions, and its festivals bring people together from all over the world. So I hope all of you can learn more about Indian culture!

Basic Facts

Official name: Republic of India, Bharat

Capital city: New Delhi

Official languages: Hindi, English

Native languages: 122 languages (21 main ones)

Religions: Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, Buddhism, Jainism, and many more

Government type: Constitutional federal democratic republic

Population: 1.417 billion

Official currency: Indian Rupee (INR)

GDP: $3.5 trillion
